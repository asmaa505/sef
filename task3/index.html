The Grand Egyptian Museum

The Grand Egyptian Museum (GEM) is Egypt’s newest and largest museum, located near the Great Pyramids of Giza.

It will officially open tomorrow and is expected to become one of the most important cultural landmarks in the world.

About the Museum

  • Located on the Giza Plateau, next to the Pyramids.
  • Built to preserve and showcase over 100,000 ancient artifacts.
  • Features modern design, wide halls, and advanced lighting systems.
  • Offers both education and entertainment for visitors of all ages.

Ticket Booking

To visit the Grand Egyptian Museum:

  1. Go to the official website: http://www.visit-gem.com/
  2. Choose your visit date and time.
  3. Select your ticket type (Adult / Student).
  4. Pay online and receive your e-ticket by email.
